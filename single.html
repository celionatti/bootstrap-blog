Summer Music Festival

Central Park, New York

Saturday, 2:00 PM - 10:00 PM

About This Event

Join us for the most anticipated music festival of the year! The Summer Music Festival brings together top artists from around the world for an unforgettable day of live performances, delicious food, and amazing atmosphere.

This year's lineup features an incredible mix of established stars and emerging talent across three stages. Experience a diverse range of musical genres from pop and rock to electronic and indie. There's something for every music lover!

In addition to the stellar musical performances, the festival offers:

  • Gourmet food vendors featuring cuisines from around the world
  • Craft beer garden with selections from local breweries
  • Art installations and interactive experiences
  • VIP areas with premium amenities and exclusive viewing spots
  • Merchandise stands featuring festival exclusives

Whether you're a festival veteran or attending your first music event, the Summer Music Festival promises an immersive experience that will create memories to last a lifetime. Don't miss out on what critics have called "the must-attend event of the summer season."

Frequently Asked Questions

Your ticket includes full-day access to all three stages and performances. Food, drinks, and merchandise are available for purchase separately. VIP tickets include access to exclusive viewing areas, complimentary refreshments, and festival merchandise.

Tickets are refundable up to 14 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to another person but are non-refundable. In case of event cancellation, full refunds will be issued automatically.

Prohibited items include outside food and beverages, large bags or backpacks, professional cameras or recording equipment, weapons of any kind, illegal substances, and pets (service animals excepted). Small personal bags, empty water bottles, sunscreen, and blankets are permitted.

Children under 10 can attend for free when accompanied by a ticket-holding adult. We recommend ear protection for young children. Please note that some performances may not be appropriate for young audiences. There is a designated family-friendly area with activities for children.
